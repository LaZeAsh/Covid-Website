Covid-19 Myths


Misinformation is dangerous. Knowing the truth about health and safety is important.

How do you check misinformation? Check the data, source, and motive.


Common Myths/Misinformation Debunked:


  • “The vaccines contain chips that will be used to track people.”
    RFID chips are large, it will not fit through the hole of a needle used for a vaccine.

  • “5G gives people COVID-19.”
    5G is a radio frequency. You cannot get viruses from radio waves. Viruses spread the most commonly through respiratory droplets when people talk, cough, or sneeze.

  • “Children cannot get COVID-19.”
    Children are not immune to viruses. Scientists believe children play a significant role in the spread of COVID-19 due to young children having poor cough etiquette, hygiene, and more.

  • “There is a 99% survival rate which means we do not need to be worried about it.”
    There’s many long term effects of this virus that we do not know very much about yet. The mortality rate increases by age but having COVID-19 for a long period of time can cause serious long term complications such as blood clots, neurological issues and damage to the heart and lungs.
