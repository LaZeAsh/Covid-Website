How to Protect your Self from Covid-19

  • Wear a Mask
  • Maintain Social Distancing (6 feet if possible)
  • Wear a mask that has no holes and is made out of multiple layers
  • Avoid going to places at rush hour
  • Wash your hands often with soap and water for at least 20 seconds especially after you have been in a public place
  • Use a hand sanitizer that contains at least 60% alcohol
  • Avoid touching your eyes, nose, and mouth
  • Avoid close contact with people who are sick
