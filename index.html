What is Covid-19?

Covid-19 is a disease caused by SARS-Cov-2. It is a respiratory disease that impacts your lungs. Covid-19 can be fatal due to much exposure to your lungs. There is no known cure but a vaccine is recievable to people aged 12 up.

History of Covid-19

In 2019 a virus was identified as Covid variant, in this case, Covid -19, popularly known as the corona virus, this virus had developed into a pandemic, that mainly affects the respiratory systems of humans, The virus is deemed to be very deadly due to the various symptoms associated with it. Some of these symptoms are as follows: Fever or chills Cough Shortness of breath or difficulty breathing Fatigue Muscle or body aches Headache New loss of taste or smell Sore throat Congestion or runny nose Nausea or vomiting Diarrhea. These symptoms may appear within 2-14 days, they can be mild or severe.
